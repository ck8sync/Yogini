About Yogini

Empowering Women & Children Through Yoga

Yogini is a dedicated online platform bringing the ancient art of power yoga to modern women and children. Our mission is to foster physical strength, mental clarity, and spiritual well-being in a supportive, virtual community.

We focus exclusively on live online classes to provide personalized attention and make wellness accessible to everyone, everywhere. Our certified instructors guide you through practices that build flexibility, reduce stress, and promote a healthy lifestyle.
